Did you know?

The first mechanical calculator was invented by Blaise Pascal in 1642. It was called the Pascaline and could perform addition and subtraction.

Many calculators are powered by solar cells, reducing the need for disposable batteries and making them environmentally friendly.

With the advent of the internet, numerous online calculators have become available, offering a wide range of functionalities accessible from any device with internet access.

The first pocket-sized calculator, the "Cal Tech," was introduced in 1971 by Texas Instruments. It revolutionized the industry, making calculations portable and accessible.
